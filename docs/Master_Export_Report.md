## 🎨 Design Philosophy: Obsidian Kinetic
These exports strictly adhere to the professional UI standards established for JIRAAssistant:
- **The "No-Line" Rule**: Boundaries are defined by tonal shifts and 64px Gaussian backdrop-blur, not solid lines.
- **Atmospheric Bloom**: Active elements emit a 40px soft light rather than traditional shadows.
- **Physical Depth**: Every panel features a 1px/2px inner-glow rim on the top and left to catch "virtual light."
